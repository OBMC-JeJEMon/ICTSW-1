Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress who gained worldwide fame with her debut album SOUR in 2021. Her breakout hit "drivers license" became a cultural phenomenon, topping charts worldwide and earning her multiple Grammy Awards. Olivia's emotional, raw lyrics about heartbreak, love, and personal growth resonated deeply with fans, especially younger audiences. She blends pop, alt-rock, and indie influences to create a sound that feels both timeless and current. Adele

Adele is a British singer-songwriter whose rich, soulful voice and emotional ballads have made her one of the best-selling artists of all time. She first gained global recognition with her 2008 debut album 19, but it was 21 (2011) that catapulted her to superstardom with hits like "Rolling in the Deep" and "Someone Like You." Known for her vulnerability and storytelling, Adele's music often centers around heartbreak and self-reflection. Her subsequent albums, 25 (2015) and 30 (2021), continued to showcase her powerful voice and mature songwriting. With multiple Grammy Awards and millions of albums sold, Adele remains one of the most respected and beloved artists in contemporary music.

wave to earth

Wave to Earth is an indie rock band known for their atmospheric, emotionally charged music. Combining elements of post-punk, shoegaze, and alternative rock, the band creates a sound that's both reflective and haunting. Their song "Seasons" is a prime example of their style, featuring dynamic guitar riffs and introspective lyrics that explore themes of change and personal growth. Wave to Earth has gained attention for their ability to blend melodic intensity with emotional depth, drawing listeners into a world where the music reflects the complexity of human experiences. With their cinematic approach to rock, they continue to build a dedicated fanbase and leave a lasting impression in the indie music scene.
